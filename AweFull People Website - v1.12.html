WHO ARE THESE AWEFULL PEOPLE?

We are a group of people who have been deeply impacted by the experiences of awe, connection, reflection and traveling. Through our work, we seek to expand these experiences and their positive effects.

Glenn Astofi

Seasoned entrepreneur and executive with a track record of success across diverse industries, including technology, financial services, brick and mortar and online retail. He has held senior leadership roles in startups and large public companies, specializing in finance, business strategy, and innovation-driven capital fundraising.

Emiliano Sigales

Multidisciplinary entrepreneur passionate about life-philosophy, travel, project development and meaningful innovation. Emiliano builds visionary projects that connect and empower others, blending digital marketing, podcasting, and technology with systems thinking and human-centered design.
